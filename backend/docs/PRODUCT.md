# Product — Scalpel

## Register

product

## Users

Surgeons in operating rooms. They interact with this tool before and during live surgery — hands may be gloved, attention is split, stakes are high. Context: pre-op prep (uploading patient data) and intra-op voice guidance (hands-free AI queries).

## Product Purpose

AI-powered live surgery assistant. Doctors upload patient history and documents pre-op, then use voice to query Scalpel during surgery for procedure steps, guidelines, and patient-specific context.

## Brand Personality

Clinical, precise, confident. Like a surgical instrument — no decoration that doesn't serve a purpose.

## Anti-references

- Cluttered medical dashboards with too many colors
- Generic SaaS landing pages
- Overly playful or casual health apps
- Glassmorphism / gradient-heavy designs

## Design Principles

1. Information density without clutter — show what matters, hide what doesn't
2. Instant scannability — a glance should surface the critical data
3. Quiet confidence — the interface recedes so the content leads
4. Zero ambiguity — every element has one clear meaning

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 contrast for text)
- Large touch targets for gloved hands on mobile
- Clear state indicators visible in bright OR lighting
- Reduced motion support
